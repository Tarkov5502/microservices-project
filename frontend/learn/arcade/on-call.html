ON-CALL

A roguelike about keeping the lights on

It's 2:47 AM. Your laptop is glowing. The pager just went off. You have a deck of cards that map to real Kubernetes and SRE tools, and a parade of production incidents are coming for you. Each run is one week as the on-call engineer. Survive four quarters. Defeat four bosses. Don't let your uptime hit zero.

Build your deck from real primitives — Liveness Probe, Pod Disruption Budget, Circuit Breaker, Multi-AZ Failover. Collect relics that represent architectural decisions. Combine them into something that survives Black Friday.
