THE ARCADE

six games that actually teach by being fun

Each game is a tangible, replayable simulation of one specific concept. No quizzes. No "click next." Real game mechanics with stakes, fail states, and scoring. The lesson lands because you played it, not because someone told you.

Defeat Black Friday, Region Outage, Security Audit, and The Acquisition. Each run teaches the combinatorics of resilience.

THE GARDENER

A parable about letting go of the hoe.

You inherit an old estate. The strict rule of the head gardener: you do not plant. You write the plan. Apprentices read your clipboard and tend the garden — replanting losses, harvesting fruit, calming after storms. You'll be tempted to do things by hand. Resist. The reconciler does what you wrote, forever.
