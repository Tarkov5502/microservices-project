    CHAOS_THEATER

    trigger real failures · watch real recovery

    This dashboard lets you trigger failure scenarios against a microservices platform and watch the system recover in real time. Every event on the right is a real Kubernetes recovery action: readiness probes, replicasets, circuit breakers, autoscalers, all working as designed.

    Click an ⚡ Action in the left sidebar to start. Or hit a ▶ Scenario to run a pre-built sequence (Black Friday, Region Disaster, Cache Cascade, etc.).

    After each action completes, the educational panel slides in with a step-by-step breakdown, the K8s primitives that responded, and what to take away.
